About Biddzy

Biddzy is your premier online auction house where you can bid on a wide variety of items, from electronics to furniture. We aim to provide a seamless and exciting bidding experience for our users.

How It Works

Participating in our auctions is easy:

  • Register for a free account and receive a $1000 bonus.
  • Browse our wide range of auctions.
  • Place your bids and try to win amazing items.
  • If you win, the item is yours. If not, the money you spent on bidding is returned to your account.

Bidding Refund Policy

At Biddzy, we believe in fair play. If you do not win an auction, the money you spent on bidding will be refunded to your account, allowing you to participate in future auctions without losing out.

Sign-Up Bonus

New users get a head start with a $1000 bonus upon registration. Use this bonus to place your bids and win fantastic items.
